Community

You are not doing this alone

Thousands of people have walked this road before you, and a lot of them are in our groups right now answering the same questions you have. The groups are free and always will be. Come in, say salam, and ask.

Our groups

The main hijrah group is where planning questions go: visas, timing, cities, costs. Members post job openings and housing leads, and brothers already in Jeddah, Makkah and Madinah answer from experience. Sisters have their own group with sister admins.

Join the main WhatsApp group

Ask in the group or message us for the sisters' group, the KSA jobs group and the country-specific groups; we add people by hand to keep the groups clean.

These are independent channels, not ours, and we cannot promise every post in them is honest. Treat job offers and housing ads there with the same care you would treat any classifieds; the jobs guide explains the checks that matter.

Group rules, so you know

Keep it clean and keep it kind. No visa trading, no "free visa" offers, no business fronting arrangements; those get people deported and they get posts deleted. Fiqh disputes go to the people of knowledge, not the group chat. And answer the newcomers patiently, because someone did it for you.
