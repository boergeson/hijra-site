Contact

Tell us your situation

We are based in Makkah and Jeddah and we answer personally. WhatsApp is fastest. Salam alaikum first, then the details below, and we can actually help from the first message.

What to include

Your nationality and where you live now. Who is coming: just you, or a wife and kids and their ages. Your profession or field, and your education. Roughly what you can work with financially, because the honest answer about routes depends on it. And your timeline: this year, next year, or "when Allah opens the way".

With that, we can usually tell you in the first reply which of the four doors realistically fits, what to prepare, and what our fee would be if you want us to run the process with you. The information is free. The fee is for the work: applications, follow-ups, phone calls in Arabic, checking contracts, finding the apartment, meeting you at the airport.

What we do not do

We do not sell visas; nobody legally can, and anyone who says otherwise is lying to you. We do not arrange "free visa" sponsorships or Saudi-name business fronts. We do not promise outcomes that depend on a ministry's decision. What we promise is straight information, real work and honesty about your chances, and we ask Allah to put barakah in it.

A private service run by Muslims in Saudi Arabia. Not a government agency. Not affiliated with any ministry or the official Guests of God program.
