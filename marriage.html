Service

Marriage, for people building the same life

Half the questions in our groups are about hijrah. The other half, honestly, are about marriage. Many brothers and sisters want both: a spouse who is serious about the deen and serious about living in a Muslim land. We run a private marriage service inside our community for exactly these people.

How it works

You send us a written profile. We use a standard format: your background, education, work, practice of the deen, family situation, what you are looking for, and your plans for hijrah. Nothing goes anywhere without your permission, and sisters' profiles are handled with a wali involved from the first message. That is not negotiable, it is how this is done properly.

We look for fit within our network: people whose profiles we hold, members of our groups across the UK, Europe, America and the Gulf, and families we know here in Saudi Arabia. When there is a serious candidate on both sides, we introduce the families and step back. The talking, the questions, the istikharah, the decision: that is yours and your families'. We are matchmakers, not gatekeepers.

What we ask of you

Honesty in the profile, seriousness of intent, and patience. We only introduce people we would stand behind to a family we respect, which means we say no to profiles that are vague about work, status or intentions. If you have been divorced, say so. If you have children, say so. The people worth marrying respect the truth.

Why through us and not an app? Because a profile in our system comes with context: a community that knows the person, references that can be called, and a shared plan for where life is heading. An app gives you photos and a bio. A family here gives you a person their neighbours can speak for.
