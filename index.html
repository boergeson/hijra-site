The honest picture first. Saudi Arabia does not hand out residency easily. For an ordinary Muslim family there are four realistic doors: a job offer, a university scholarship, a real licensed business, or money (Premium Residency). Everything else, including the tourist visa, is a visit. The good news is that every one of these doors is more open in 2026 than it has ever been, and we can tell you exactly what each one costs.

What is new in 2026

Two changes matter a lot for hijrah right now. First, since January 2026 Muslims who are not Saudi citizens can buy property in approved zones of Makkah and Madinah, something that was closed for decades. Second, all university applications for foreigners now run through one government portal, with full scholarships that cover tuition, housing, healthcare, flights and a monthly stipend. We explain both on the housing and study pages.
