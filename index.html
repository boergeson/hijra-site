The honest picture first. Saudi Arabia does not hand out residency easily. For an ordinary Muslim family there are four realistic doors: a job offer, a university scholarship, a real licensed business, or money (Premium Residency). Everything else, including the tourist visa, is a visit. The good news is that every one of these doors is more open in 2026 than it has ever been, and we can tell you exactly what each one costs.
